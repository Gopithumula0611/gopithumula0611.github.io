About Me

Hello! I'm Gopi Thumula, a dedicated and results-driven Java Full Stack Developer with over 4 years of experience in crafting scalable, high-performance web applications. Currently, I hold a Master's degree in Computer Science from Sacred Heart University (GPA: 3.8), where I had the privilege to work as a Graduate Teaching Assistant and contribute to cutting-edge research on machine learning-based malware detection, which was published at IEEE ICSPIS 2024.

Throughout my career, I've had the opportunity to work in a variety of environments, including fintech at Mastercard, academic research, and enterprise software development at Cognizant. In each of these roles, I’ve built and delivered modern web applications using technologies such as Java 11/17, Spring Boot, Microservices, React.js, Angular, and leading cloud platforms like AWS, GCP, and Azure.

I specialize in:

  • End-to-end development of scalable, secure, and responsive applications
  • Microservices architecture, CI/CD, and cloud-native deployments
  • Integrating Machine Learning with Java-based tools for cybersecurity applications
  • Front-end development with modern JavaScript frameworks like React and Angular
  • Collaborating in Agile/Scrum teams and mentoring peers to deliver quality software

I am certified as an AWS Developer Associate and an Oracle Certified Java Programmer, and I am always excited to learn new technologies. Whether I’m building innovative web platforms or contributing to impactful research, I strive to bring a unique blend of creativity, technical depth, and business focus to everything I do.
