About Me

Hello! I'm Gopi Thumula, a passionate and skilled Java Full-Stack Developer based in Bridgeport, CT. With over 3 years of experience in software development, I specialize in building scalable backend systems and responsive front-end interfaces using modern technologies like Java, Spring Boot, React.js, Angular, and AWS.

I recently earned my Master’s degree in Computer Science from Sacred Heart University (GPA: 3.8/4.0), where I also worked as a Graduate Teaching Assistant and published research on machine learning-based malware detection at IEEE ICSPIS 2024.

Professionally, I’ve contributed to high-impact projects at Mastercard, where I helped modernize legacy systems using microservices, React.js, and cloud-native architectures. My previous experience at Cognizant included enhancing banking platforms with secure APIs, Dockerized deployments, and real-time data processing using Kafka.

My technical toolkit includes Java 17, Spring Boot, REST APIs, Kafka, Docker, Kubernetes, MongoDB, AWS, React, Angular, and CI/CD tools like Jenkins and CircleCI. I’m also certified as an AWS Developer Associate and Oracle Certified Java Programmer.

Outside of coding, I'm a problem-solver who enjoys taking on challenges, optimizing systems, and contributing to innovative tech solutions. My journey—from a small farming background to building enterprise-grade systems—reflects my dedication, resilience, and hunger to grow in the tech world.
